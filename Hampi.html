I started my trip to Hampi from Pune,Maharashtra. I went to Hampi along with my office collegue,our plan was to go to Azra and buy some rice and come back to Pune by the end of the day. But on the way we got a crazy idea of going to Hampi and we started our journey to Hampi.We travelled from Pune to Hampi and it was 12 hour journey, we had lunch in Kolhapur amd tea in Karnataka border.We reached Hampi at 8pm. I booked a Banana tree resort for our stay,it was really nice to spend a night, in a farm resort. We were drained out of energy and had a sound sleep and next day we woke up early to see the sunrise from Matanga Hill.

Sunrise from Matunga Hill


We treked to the top of Matunga Hill it took us around 30 minutes to reach the top.Whwn we was at the top the sun started raising from the horizon.The views were spectacular.
